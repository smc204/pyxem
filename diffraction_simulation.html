Electron Diffraction Simulations¶

Electron diffraction simulations are made available through pyXem.

Ultimately multiple simulation tools will be made available covering various applications but at present only the simplest methods are implemented. All methods are available through the ElectronDiffractionGenerator class.

Kinematical Simulation¶

Kinematical simulation of spot diffraction patterns is implemented as described in numerous textbooks.
